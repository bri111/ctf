🕵️ CTF Challenge: Robots.txt Recon

Web developers often forget that robots.txt is publicly readable and can accidentally reveal hidden directories. Check the paths that search engines are told to avoid. Try navigating to #/backup/ or #/secret-files/ in the URL to discover what's hiding on this server.
